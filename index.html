Karen Kingsbury

#1 New York Times Bestselling Author

Karen Kingsbury is a #1 New York Times and USA Today best selling novelist with the last dozen books published topping bestseller lists.

Here is a time line of Karen Kingsbury's life:

  • 1963-Born in Farifax, Virginia
  • 1989Married Don Russell
  • 1991-Wrote her first book, "Missy's Murder"
  • 2002-Wrote the first book in the Redemption series with Gary Smalley, "Redemption"
  • 2003-Wrote the second and third books in the Redemption series with Gary Smalley, "Remember" and "Return
  • 2004-Wrote the fourth and fifth books in the Redemption series with Gary Smalley, "Rejoice" and "Reunion"
  • 2005-Wrote the first and second books in the Firstborn series, "Fame" and "Forgiven"
  • 2006-Wrote the third and fourth books in the Firstborn series, "Found" and "Family"
  • 2007-Wrote the fifth book in the Firstborn series, "Forever" as well as the first and second books in the Sunrise series, "Sunrise" and "Summer"
  • 2008-Wrote the third and fourth books in the Sunrise series, "Someday" and "Sunset"
  • 2009-Wrote the first and second books in the Above the Line series, "Take One" and "Take Two"
  • 2010-Wrote the third and fourth books in the Above the Line series, "Take Three" and "Take Four"
  • 2011-Wrote the first, second, and third books in the Baily Flanigan series, "Leaving", "Learning", and "Longing"
  • 2012-Wrote the fourth book in the Baily Flanigan series, "Loving" as well as the book "Coming Home, The Baxter Family"
  • 2016-Wrote the first book in the Baxter Family Collection, "A Baxter Family Christmas"
  • 2017-Wrote the second and third book in the Baxter Family Collection,"Love Story" and "In This Moment"
  • 2018-Wrote the fourth and fifth book in the Baxter Family Collection, "To the Moon and Back" and "When We Were Young"
  • 2019-Wrote the sixth book in the Baxter Family Collection, "Two Weeks" as well as the first book in the Baxter Family Children series (with Tyler Russell, for ages 8-12), "Best Family Ever"
  • 2020-Wrote the seventh and eighth book in the Baxter Family Collection, "Someone Like You" and "Truly, Madly, Deeply"
  • 2021-Wrote the second book in Baxter Family Children series (with Tyler Russell, for ages 8-12), "Finding Home"
  • 2024- Her book, "Someone Like You" was made into a movie
